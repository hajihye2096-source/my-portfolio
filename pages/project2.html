2024 Q4

Background

Coupang Eats had introduced various discount coupons, but both download rates (48%) and actual usage rates (7%) fell short of expectations. While customers were aware of the coupons' existence, they had difficulty clearly understanding whether "this coupon would actually apply to my order" or "under what conditions can I use it." This confusion was due to unclear information delivery about coupons and a mismatch between the actual usage experience and the guidance provided.

Goal

User Goal

Users should clearly understand coupon conditions and application status to build trust in coupon usage

Business Goal

Increase coupon download rates and actual usage rates by improving user trust and understanding

Insights

Customers recognized the existence of coupons but couldn't gain trust due to unclear specific conditions and application methods. This likely led to customers not completing their orders.

Insufficient coupon information

Despite customers knowing about coupons, key information like usage conditions, applicability, and expiration dates beyond just the discount amount wasn't sufficiently communicated.

Confusion in coupon application process

Downloaded coupons weren't automatically applied at the cart stage, leading customers to wonder "I definitely got the coupon, why can't I use it?" and lose trust.

Trust issues leading to abandonment

Due to this uncertainty, customers hesitate to complete orders or ultimately abandon at the payment stage, even when they have coupons.

Problem & Hypothesis

01

Only coupon amount shown, conditions·applicability·expiration unclear

→If we display key coupon information (conditions·applicability·expiration) in an integrated card format, customers will understand coupon details clearly.

02

Not automatically applied in cart after download

→If we provide immediate guidance on auto-download and applicability, customers will trust the coupon application process.

03

Difficult to confirm 'coupon applied state'

→If we provide clear visual indication of discount application completion, customers will feel confident about their savings.

Solution Direction

We made coupon information instantly understandable and immediately verifiable, ensuring customers feel confident they're in a 'guaranteed discount state.'

Only coupon amount shown, conditions·applicability·expiration unclear

↓

Integrated display of core coupon information (conditions·applicability·expiration) in card format

Not automatically applied in cart after download

↓

Immediate guidance on auto-download and applicability

Difficult to confirm 'coupon applied state'

↓

Clear visual indication of discount application completion

User Test

Can users clearly understand coupon conditions and application status to build trust in coupon usage?

Figma interactive prototype testing, interviews and scenario-based task execution (discover–understand–apply)

Result

3 out of 4 participants successfully understood coupon conditions and felt confident about using them

Reflection

1. The core of this project wasn't just showing coupons, but helping customers truly understand coupon benefits.

2. Through this process, I learned that customers only trust benefits when they have sufficient information.

3. This project reinforced that clear information structure is the first step in building trust.
