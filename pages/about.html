Work About Résumé

Hi, I'm Jihye, a product designer building thoughtful systems and cultures.

I'm currently designing for Coupang Eats, where I create intuitive experiences that make food delivery feel effortless and personal.

My recent work includes launching Coupang Eats Japan, designing AI chat experiences, and improving CRM in-app support flows for better customer communication.

 

I believe design is not just about solving problems, but about creating the culture that helps people solve them together.

Team & Community Moments

Beyond design work, I care deeply about how teams grow together.
I've hosted UX book clubs, organized year-end workshops, and built study groups that help designers learn and connect beyond their roles. These small, human moments remind me that good design starts with good culture.

Life & Curiosity

Outside of work, I draw inspiration from quiet, everyday moments! Painting soft colors on canvas, walking my dog at sunset, or sharing long meals with friends.
These small rituals keep me grounded and remind me why I design? To bring warmth and connection into daily life.
